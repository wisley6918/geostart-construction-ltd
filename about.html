Company Overview

Geostart Constructions Limited is a privately owned construction company based in Kisumu, Kenya. We specialize in building construction, civil engineering works, infrastructure development, renovation, and project management services.

Our company is built on integrity, transparency, accountability, and excellence. We aim to deliver high-quality construction projects that meet client expectations and contribute to Kenya’s development goals.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable and innovative construction partner in East Africa, delivering landmark projects that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, safe, and sustainable construction projects using skilled personnel, modern techniques, and strict quality control.
